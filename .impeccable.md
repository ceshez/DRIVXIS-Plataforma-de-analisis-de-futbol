## Design Context

### Users
Coaches, analysts, and academy staff reviewing football videos to extract tactical insights and prepare sessions. They need dense, clear information and fast access to uploads, statuses, and tactical views.

### Brand Personality
Direct, technical, and competitive. The product should feel sharp, serious, and high-performance, not playful.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark-first interface with orange-black identity, high contrast typography, strong section hierarchy, and restrained visual effects. Avoid generic startup gradients and avoid card overload.

### Design Principles
1. Prioritize scanability for data-heavy football workflows.
2. Keep action hierarchy obvious with one dominant call to action per section.
3. Use color with intent: orange for momentum, neutrals for structure.
4. Preserve visual consistency across landing, auth, dashboard, and videos.
5. Balance polish with performance and readability on mobile and desktop.
